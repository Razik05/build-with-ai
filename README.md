# Project Name
GlobalFleetAI

## Problem Statement
Modern supply chains manage millions of concurrent shipments across complex, volatile transportation networks. When disruptions such as weather events, port congestion, carrier failures occur they are identified only after delivery timelines are already compromised. There is no intelligent system that continuously analyzes live transit data, predicts how localized bottlenecks will cascade, and dynamically recommends or executes optimized reroutes before the damage spreads. The result is a reactive, fragile logistics ecosystem that costs businesses billions and fails end consumers silently.

## Project Description
GlobalFleetAI is an AI-powered supply chain command center that continuously monitors thousands of live shipments across carriers, ports, and transportation networks in real time. When a disruption signal is detected — a storm, port congestion, or carrier delay — the AI doesn't just flag the affected shipment; it maps every downstream dependency and predicts exactly how the delay will cascade before it happens. It then instantly generates optimized rerouting options ranked by cost, speed, and reliability, and either recommends them to a human operator for one-click approval or executes low-risk reroutes autonomously. All affected suppliers, carriers, and customers are notified automatically with updated timelines — no manual follow-up needed. Unlike traditional tracking tools that tell you something went wrong after the fact, ChainSight tells you what will go wrong, how far it will spread, and what to do about it — before your customers ever notice.
